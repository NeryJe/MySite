Shine

Find your unique style and let it shine!

The Problem

Chalc.io helps no-code platforms create in-app tutorials. The Client aims to improve the functionality of the training builder and possible tutorial player of the desktop app, by redesigning the existing the interface making it more useful and functional for users.

The Solution

We created a fashion app that helped users to find their unique style by implementing AI and ML. We implemented a user centered approach through the design process. Based on the user stories and user flows, we designed the platform focusing on the next key features:

  • Camera, Search item, Discover style based on app suggestion.
  • Implemented AI for‘live try on’clothing items.
  • Style suggestions based on likes, and Brands recommend .

Role

UX/UI Designer on a team of 5 Designers.

Duration:

5 Weeks

Discovery

Kick off meeting

In our first meeting we prepared ideal questions for the client to learn more about the functionality for this app, as well to know more about the desired aspects of design. The meeting opened the doors to share my thoughts with the team as well as helping me process and understand the client’s needs.

Color Exploration

Client did not have a color pallet established, so I created a color wash to select colors based on what the client described she was looking for. We presented the colors to the client and she chose the colors based on the purple heart.

Moodboard

Afterwards I continue exploring and working on the brand definition. The interface inspiration was chosen based on creating an app where users can feel creative and unique. The client wants an app that looks cheerful and empowering for the users.

Ideation

User Stories

Based on the client MVP's, the users stories was created. The client chose 2 High Priority and 2 as Medium Priority out of 10 stories.

High Priority

  • As a user, I want to be able to take a picture of a piece of clothing (or type in its unique attributes) and receive details on its type, style category, and where I can buy it, so I can easily purchase clothes I like.
  • As a user, I want a platform that will provide me with clothing and business suggestions similar to my likes/saves and search history, so that I can discover new clothing items similar to what I like.

Medium Priority

  • As a user, I want a discovery page that features new items of clothing, new businesses, etc, so that it is tailored to my unique fashion style.
  • As a user, I want to preview what clothes would look like on me before I buy them (AI might be able to do this?), so that I know how the clothing will look and so the (oftentimes annoying) process of returning clothes is avoided.

User Flows

Based on User Stories the next step was to create user flows from the 4 users stories that have been selected.

Sketches

Sketching helps us bring a lot of ideas together to have a better perspective guiding us to find possible solutions. Taking into account the user stories and user flows, it helped us sketch our ideas for the interface of the app.

Design

Design Library

For the design library I helped creating the social CTAs and Icons which helped us with consistency and ease throughout the design process.

Low Fidelity Wireframes

At this point we have everything we need to start working on wireframes. I mainly focus on the welcome page, home page, and item description. Once the team had finished with their wireframes I helped by checking that everything was consistent using the 8 point grid system.

Client Feedback

After reviewing the wireframe, the client was not yet satisfied with the results. She asked us to iterate the following:

  1. Rearranging the home page, instead of being the discovery page of items, she wants the users to start searching for the item they want by typing or taking a picture of the item.
  2. Add the style swiper like the dating app tinder, where the user can play, build, and discover their style.

Design Decisions

In the next step I iterated the search and camera features. The first designs didn’t seem to be consistent with the other pages; the icons and search bar weren't working for us in the sense of calling the user to take action.

I took a more minimalist approach by adding more neat icons for the camera, the search bar was improved by arranging the position of the icon and text label.

Wireframe 2nd iteration

Now that we have solved the home page, swiper style, search, and camera design. We sent our second iteration to the client and she was very satisfied with the results, she loved the aesthetics and functionality of the wireframe.

UI Iteration

The next step was for each member of the team to create a possible final UIand for the home page and discovery page. I worked on the number 3, which I decided to add some light purple in the nav bar, and dark blue for the rest of the buttons/icons. Once we finalized our part we all got together and chose the ones we liked, and each of us explained why we liked the screens we chose. We finalized by picking 3 out of 5 designs, in which mine has been chosen by the team. Afterwards, the client decided to go for the number 2 because she felt the colors were just right and loved the look overall.

High Fidelity

After having the client decide on the two screens we started working on the final UI. The client was delighted with the end results.

Style Guide

We decided to work on the style guides last. Once we had the high fidelity we decided to go back and add the rest of the elements.

Test

Prototype

Last step for our project was creating a prototype so that the product can be tested.

I helped create this prototype, after it was done I let my team test it and give feedback, they approved it, then we moved on with sending it to the client. She was very pleased and satisfied since this will help for further development of the product.

Lookin back

Having my first project with a team showed me how important communication is. We had very clear communication overall, and the client was understandable when sharing her ideas with us.

I learned that the design process can change at any point. The process sometimes won’t be as expected, but working as a team and coming up with a solution was very satisfying.

Looking forward

This project has much potential, I would have liked to have added some features like quizzes where it can be helpful for the user to build their styles with AI/ML to be able to recognize the user's personal style.
